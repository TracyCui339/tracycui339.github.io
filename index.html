Why Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)

Discover the steps MESD takes to drive sustainable change in the maritime industry.

Tracking of GHG emission allows companies to assess and mitigate their carbon emission contribution.

LCA can cover direct and indirect emissions: Scope 1, 2 and 3.

LCA is applicable to quantify any products and various category impacts.

LCA considers comprehensive emissions to air, to water, to soil and to waste treatment. Including biogenic carbon.
